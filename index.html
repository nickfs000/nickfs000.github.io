Dodger Stadium on Saturday, July 15th

Dodger Stadium

Saturday, July 15th | DAY 1
2:30 PM - Auto Gates Open
3:30 PM - Doors Open
5:00 PM - Show Begins - Featuring Eagles, Steely Dan and The Doobie Brothers

More Info


Dodger Stadium Express
The Dodger Stadium Express service from Union Station will start at 3:00 pm and will run continuously throughout the first half of the concert with outbound service starting at 9:30 pm and continuing until 45 minutes after the end of the show. The service is complimentary with a Classic West concert ticket.

UBER
Arrival - Uber Vehicles may enter any of the (5) five Dodger Auto gates to drop-off passengers on the ring road.
Departure - Uber Vehicles will be located in Lot 11 below Sunset Gate A. Departing Uber vehicles will be waiting in the designated Uber Zone. Uber vehicles will send their respective aisle/stall number to passengers waiting in the queue.
